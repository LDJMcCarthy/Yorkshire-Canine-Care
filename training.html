DOG TRAINING

At Yorkshire Canine Care, our dog training is all about building understanding, confidence, and calm focus — for both you and your dog.

Every dog is different, so our sessions are fully tailored to their personality, learning style, and progress, ensuring each one gets the time and attention they need to succeed.

Dog Training

Our Dog Training service is broken down into packages, where we don’t stick rigidly to the clock.
Instead, each session runs for as long as your dog stays engaged — typically between 30 minutes and an hour.
The first session is always slightly longer, giving us time to get to know your dog, understand your goals, and create a personalised training plan.

If a session needs to end early because your dog has reached their limit, that’s no problem — any remaining time simply rolls over to your next visit.
The aim is always to finish on a positive note, helping your dog associate training with fun and success.

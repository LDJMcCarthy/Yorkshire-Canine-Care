Top Tip for Pet Parents!

Always check that your dog’s home boarder is fully licensed by the local council. A licence means the carer has passed checks on safety, cleanliness, experience, insurance, and animal welfare standards — helping to keep your dog happy, healthy, and secure while you’re away.

HOME BOARDING

At Yorkshire Canine Care, we understand that leaving your dog while you’re away can be a difficult decision.That’s why our home boarding service is designed to give your dog a true home-from-home experience — where they’ll be treated as part of our family from the moment they arrive.

We have a unique policy of only boarding one dog at a time, or two from the same household. This means your dog receives our full attention, comfort, and care throughout their stay. We take the time to help each dog settle in, ensuring they feel calm, happy, and secure in our home environment.

Yorkshire Canine Care is fully insured, DBS-checked, professionally qualified, and registered with the local council. We hold the Highest Council Award for our home boarding standards, so you can have complete confidence that your dog is in safe, capable hands.

Each day includes countryside walks to provide both physical exercise and mental stimulation. We also include shorter local walks and engaging play sessions tailored to your dog’s needs, keeping them relaxed and entertained throughout their stay.

This gives you the opportunity to meet us, view our qualifications, DBS check, registration and insurance documents, and discuss your dog’s routine and personality in detail. It’s a great way to ensure both you and your dog feel comfortable before their stay.

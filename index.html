Our Services

Yorkshire Canine Care is a family-run business driven by a simple yet profound mission to provide a comprehensive range of services tailored to the diverse needs of dog owners.

We operate in and around Barwick in Elmet, Aberford, Scholes, Garforth and Micklefield. If you are in the area and need a help with your pooches, give us a call on 07881 211945.

MEET OUR TEAM

Our team, a group of seasoned professionals, not only possess a wealth of experience but also share a genuine love for dogs. Coupled with our commitment to deliver outstanding service, it ensures that your furry friend is in the best hands.

WARREN

Warren, the founder of Yorkshire Canine Care, brings a unique blend of military precision and a deep passion for canine companionship to his approach. This distinctive combination, honed over 20 years of military experience, results in top-notch services for your furry friends. His unparalleled discipline, reliability, and leadership skills seamlessly translate into exceptional dog care.

For over 24 years, Warren has worked with dogs of all breeds, gaining invaluable hands-on experience and developing an intuitive understanding of canine behaviour. This extensive background is complemented by a suite of professional qualifications, including:

Level 3 Award in Dog Walking
Level 3 Award in Pet Sitting
Level 3 Award in Canine Emergency First Aid
Level 3 Certificate in Dog Grooming
Level 3 Certificate in Dog Day Care and Home Boarding
Certified Dog Trainer (DTC)

KAIYA

With over 10 years of experience working with animals and a background as a trained Medical Technician, Kaiya brings expertise, care, and attention to detail to every grooming session.

As our Dog Grooming Salon Manager, Kaiya ensures each dog enjoys a calm, positive, and professional grooming experience — tailored to their individual needs and comfort. Kaiya’s passion for animal welfare and her commitment to high standards make her an invaluable part of the Yorkshire Canine Care team. Her friendly and professional approach ensures every dog leaves looking and feeling their best. She holds the following qualifications, making her exceptionally equipped to handle dogs of all breeds and temperaments:

Level 3 Award in Canine Emergency First Aid
Level 3 Certificate in Dog Grooming

GEORGIA

Having moved into the area leaving South West Cornwall, Georgia hopes to bring her experience to the business and continue to pursue Dog welfare and behaviour.

Georgia has worked with a variety of animals all her life, then found a passion for animal behaviour, especially canine behaviour following working in a family kennels. She went on to study animals through college and university gaining much experience and knowledge. She then went on to work in animal rescue training and rehoming dogs and cats for over 3 years. Working with a variety of breeds and behavioural issues.

Georgia wants to bring her experience with behavioural study and training rescue into Yorkshire Canine care. Bringing patience, confidence and consistency to any of your pets physical or behavioural needs. With the following qualifications:
Level 3 Advanced technical Diploma in Animal Management
BSc Zoology with Animal behaviour with Placement year

BETHANY

Bethany is a dedicated animal care professional with a Level 3 qualification in Animal Care with hands-on experience working in a kennel environment. She has experience caring for dogs of various breeds, supporting their daily routines, and maintaining high standards of welfare and cleanliness.

With a genuine love for all animals big and small, Bethany is passionate about providing calm, reliable, and attentive care. Her caring nature and commitment to animal wellbeing make her a valuable member of any animal care team and perfect to look after your furry friends.

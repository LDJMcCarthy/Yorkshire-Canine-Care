GROOMING

Our upcoming Dog Grooming service at Yorkshire Canine Care is designed to keep your canine companion looking and feeling their absolute best. From basic bath and dry to full grooming sessions, our experienced groomers will provide personalised care tailored to your dog's specific needs. Services will include coat clipping and styling, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and teeth cleaning, ensuring your dog remains healthy and happy. Our state-of-the-art grooming facility will offer a calm and welcoming environment, reducing stress and making the grooming experience enjoyable for your pet. Whether your dog needs a quick touch-up or a complete makeover, our professional grooming team is here to pamper your pooch from head to tail. Stay tuned for the launch of our grooming services and treat your dog to the ultimate grooming experience.

TRAINING

Yorkshire Canine Care will soon offer comprehensive Dog Training services to help you build a strong, positive relationship with your furry friend. Whether you're facing specific behavioural challenges or want to enhance your dog's obedience skills, our expert trainers will provide the guidance and techniques necessary for success. We understand that every dog is unique, which is why our training sessions will be available in both group classes and one-on-one settings, allowing for flexibility and personalised attention. Keep an eye out for our upcoming training services and take the first step towards a well-behaved and happy dog.

HOME BOARDING

Are you planning a holiday or a weekend getaway? Our Home Boarding service at Yorkshire Canine Care will provide your dog with a safe, comfortable, and loving environment while you're away. Unlike traditional kennels, our home boarding service offers a cosy, home-like setting where your dog can enjoy all the comforts they are used to, along with plenty of walks, attention and care from our dedicated team. Your dog will have access to supervised playtime, comfortable sleeping arrangements, and lots of cuddles and companionship. We will ensure your pet follows their regular routine, including feeding schedules, exercise, and any special requirements they may have. Our home boarding service is perfect for dogs of all ages and sizes, providing peace of mind for you and a delightful stay for your furry friend. Watch this space for the launch of our home boarding service and give your dog a home-away-from-home experience.

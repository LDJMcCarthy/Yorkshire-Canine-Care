Dog Day Care

Our Dog Day Care service provides a safe, fun, home-from-home stimulating environment for your dog from 8am to 5pm. Depending on your dog’s age, ability, and energy levels, we offer two one-hour countryside walks to meet their physical and mental stimulation needs. For dogs requiring lighter activity, we have a secure garden where they can engage in playtime, basic training, light exercise, and mental stimulation.

We cater to dogs of all ages and abilities, ensuring that each one receives the individual attention they deserve. Local walks are also available when needed, and we treat your dog with the same love and care we give our pets.

At Yorkshire Canine Care, your dog’s well-being is our top priority. If you have any questions about our Daycare service, feel free to contact us—we’re always happy to help!

GROUP WALK
60 MINUTES
£15

Our off-lead group walks are designed to give your dog the exercise, stimulation, and socialisation they need to live a happy, balanced life.

Each walk lasts 60 minutes, where we stimulate your furry friends both mentally and physically in a safe and controlled environment. Dogs are carefully matched based on their temperament, energy levels, and play style to ensure a fun and positive experience for everyone.

We collect your dog from home, take them on exciting group adventures, and return them happy, clean, and ready for a well-earned rest.

Whether your dog loves running, playing, or simply exploring with friends, our group walks provide the perfect balance of fun, exercise, and companionship.

To ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all dogs in our care, we ask that:
Your dog has a good level of recall.
Your dog does not show aggression or resource guarding behaviours.
Your dog is comfortable walking off lead and socialising with other dogs.

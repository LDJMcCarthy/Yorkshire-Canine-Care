Our Prices

Our grooming prices are based on the height of your dog, ensuring a fair and accurate cost for the time, care, and attention each groom requires.

Taller dogs typically need longer appointment slots, more product use, and additional handling to achieve a safe, high-quality finish.

By using height as our guide, we’re able to offer transparent pricing that reflects the individual needs of each dog while maintaining the highest standards of comfort and care during their groom.

Additional Services

Does your four legged friend, require some extra care? When booking your package, feel free to ammend the following services:

Teeth Cleaning | £20

Regular teeth cleaning helps keep your dog’s mouth healthy and their breath fresh. We use gentle, effective methods to remove plaque and tartar build-up, reducing the risk of gum disease and discomfort. A clean mouth means a happier, healthier dog.

De-matting | £15

Matted fur can be painful and cause skin irritation if left untreated. Our de-matting service carefully removes tangles and knots while keeping your dog as comfortable as possible. We work gently to restore a smooth, healthy coat that looks great and feels even better.

Ear Cleaning | £5

Clean ears are essential for your dog’s overall health. We gently remove dirt, wax, and debris to help prevent infections and irritation. Regular ear cleaning keeps your dog comfortable and can reduce the risk of unpleasant odours or ear problems.

Nail Clipping | £10

Keeping your dog’s nails trimmed is important for their comfort and mobility. Our nail clipping service ensures nails are cut safely and to the correct length, preventing splits, overgrowth, and discomfort when walking.

Tidy Up | £15

A tidy-up is perfect for maintaining your dog’s appearance between full grooms. We trim around the face, paws, and hygiene areas to keep them looking neat and well cared for. It’s a quick refresh that helps your dog stay clean, comfortable, and smart.

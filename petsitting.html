PET SITTING

Our pet sitting service provides dependable, one-to-one care for your pets while you’re away, allowing them to remain comfortable and relaxed in familiar surroundings.
Whether you’re heading off on holiday, away overnight, or working long hours, you can trust that your pets are being well looked after.

This service is ideal for dogs, cats, and small animals who prefer the security of home and their usual routine.

Pet Sitting

All pet sitting bookings are tailored to suit your pet’s individual needs and routine.

Services can include:

Feeding and fresh water.
Toilet breaks and walks (if required).
Playtime, companionship, and enrichment.
Administration of medication where needed.
Cleaning of bowls, litter trays, and pet areas.
Maintaining your pet’s normal daily routine.
Regular updates and photos for your peace of mind.

We take the time to understand your pet, their behaviour, and any specific requirements before your booking begins.

We offer experienced, reliable, and fully insured pet sitting with friendly local carers who provide calm, one-to-one attention in your pet’s own home — never kennels or catteries — helping to reduce stress. With flexible visit options including overnight stays, your pet’s comfort, welfare, and happiness are always our top priority.

Our pet sitting service is ideal for dogs who are more settled at home, cats who dislike travelling or unfamiliar environments, puppies needing routine and supervision, senior pets requiring extra care, and small animals that need reliable daily checks — all cared for in the comfort of their own home.

Meet and Greet
All pet sitting bookings begin with a free meet & greet. This allows us to meet you and your pet, discuss routines, access arrangements, and answer any questions — ensuring everything is in place before you go away.

If you’re looking for a trustworthy and professional pet sitting service, please get in touch to discuss your requirements or to arrange your free meet & greet.
